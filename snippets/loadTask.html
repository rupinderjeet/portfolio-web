A complicated Google Form for tracking and sending Queries / Issues / Suggestions to their respective handling departments based on the selection an End-User makes in the end. And, Submitted forms will send the responses to a predestined e-mail recipient.

My Client was looking for an easy way to handle and solve the queries of the employees of their company. They didn't wanted to face every individual employee, ask his problem and provide the solution.

So, he requested for a Form that can be filled by anyone to contact them. This form was used to collect information from the employee about his query and his contact details. After filling out the form correctly, the data gets stored in a Google Spreadsheets.

According to selection made by the End-User, the form data gets sent to respective department. For e.g. if a person selects a question regarding salary, the form data gets mailed to HR mailbox and if the person selects a question regarding invoices, the form data gets mailed to the Business department.

In this way, they were notified as soon as a new enquiry was registered.

Later on, I added more support for sending Suggestions through forms.
