Hello, I'm Rupinderjeet Singh. I was born in Punjab, India. I live with my mother and sister. My dad paid for my study very well, but unfortunately, we couldn't get along after studies.


When I was little, I loved drawing and computing very much. I could never get tired from both. This shaped my interest in the Programming and 2D drawing eventually lead to my studies in Computer Science. I acquired a Bachelor of Technology degree from a local university, known as PTU, in Computer Science Engineering. I was not so happy with theory-content alone, so i started learning how to program from any source i could get. read more..


In 2012, I got my first computer(Dual Core Pentium, 2.70Ghz, 2GB RAM, 320GB HDD). Ever since then, I was attached with Java programming language. In 2014, I got my second computer(actually a Laptop, Dell Inspiron 3542, current PC).


I developed my programming and designing skills randomly(whenever i got extra time) while working as a Back Office Computer Operator(Data Entry) in Dada Motors(Tata Motors Dealer), Locally. In my spare time, I enjoy taking photographs so that I can reinterpret nature's beauty using my point of view. I also enjoy listening improvised music that cannot be put in words. It is my belief that this world can be portrayed in a hundred different ways – the things that we see, the places we go, and the people that we meet.
