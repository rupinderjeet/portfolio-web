Anonymous Camera Privacy Policy

Anonymous Camera helps you to take pictures secretly through some easy-to-use shortcuts. The application itself doesn't record or track the activity of user. System camera is used to take pictures and record videos only, this user-generated content belongs to user himself, not to AC. By using this application, you agree and acknowledge that you are solely responsible for obtaining the consent of any thing you record, if or not such consent is required by applicable law.


Permissions

  • Camera  : AC uses system camera to take pictures (and videos in future) only.
  • Storage  : AC uses Storage permission to save images taken using camera.
  • Internet : AC uses Internet to display Advertisements.
  • Overlay  : AC uses Overlay permission on Marshmallow 6.0.0 to open system camera.

Information Collection

  • AC does not collect any kind of "personal" or non-personal information. The user isn't required to provide LogIn or Email to use any functionality. However, if you "Send Feedback" from the application, AC tries to fetch device's Manufacturer Name, Device Model Number and Android version. It is used to keep track of Tested devices. This information can be, easily, removed by user from message before sending feedback. Other than this, AC does not collect any kind of information.

Advertisement

  • AC uses AdMob ad services to show advertisements from AdMob Network. This Network is proven safe and follows Google's Ad Policy. AC shows banner ads only. There are no full-screen ads in the app. If the user purchases The Gift of Charity app from Google play store, these ads will disappear. In future, for small amount of money, these ads can be removed from the app.

Future Updates

  • This privacy policy can be changed at anytime to reflect any change or statement. Any changes in this policy document will be considered as a part of the document at all times.
