Why is Total Reach more than Total number of visitors?

Total Reach is incremented whenever a user lands on this website. But, Visitor count is incremented whenever a visitor marks his attendance on this page. Out of all the people browsing this website, only 30% of them come to this page without getting bored. These special people leave their foot trails by marking their attendance. Also, during development, this page is refreshed many times in a day.
